Q1. What is GlucoDock®?

GlucoDock® is a blood glucose monitoring module to detect the glucose level in human blood. GlucoDock® can be used in combination with iPhone (3GS and newer generations), iPod touch (3. Generation and newer) or iPad (all generations) and the VitaDock® App.

Q2. Is GlucoDock® a certified medical device?

Yes, GlucoDock® is certified by an authorized organization in accordance to the directive IVD 98/79/EG.

Q3. Which devices are compatible with GlucoDock®?

You can use GlucoDock® with your iPhone (3GS and newer generations), iPod touch (3. Generation and newer) and iPad (all generations).

Q4. Can the GlucoDock® blood glucose monitoring module be used by multiple people?

You are able to connect up to 4 user accounts.

Q5. Does GlucoDock® require a separate power supply?

No, the connected iPhone, iPod touch or iPad supplies GlucoDock® with power.

Q6. Can the measuring unit within GlucoDock® be adjusted individually?

Of course. The VitaDock® App allows personal user settings, such as to determine measuring unites mmol/L or mg/dL.

Q7. Which test trips do I need for measuring with my GlucoDock®?

For the GlucoDock® glucose measuring module you need the GlucoDock® test strips.

Q8. Can I use other test strips with GlucoDock®?

No. GlucoDock® can only be used with GlucoDock® test strips designed by Medisana.

Q9. Can I also use GlucoDock® with an Android device?

Due to technical restrictions GlucoDock® cannot be connected to an Android device.
